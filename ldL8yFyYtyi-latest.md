<undefined><li>**<u>Ewald from GridSingularity (Clément)</u>**</li></undefined>

_<u>Context</u>_: 38mns call20 stealth workshop world wild in energy. Chili. Georgia etc.  20 business model prototypes tested to see what approach would be the best for an ever accelerating changing industry.

_<u>Team</u>_: Gav and Ethercore are building the tech needed for the future of Grid Singularity.

_<u>Roadmap</u>_: Closing founding next week. First plugging in the weeks to come March next year end of Beta mode.

_<u>Hardware part</u>_: Building a device open source to add to any power plant or substation to deal with key management protocols. They mark the meters from production to utility.

_<u>Partners</u>_: Working with Slockit, Transactive and other actors. 

Working to build a Utility Consortium.

_<u>Events</u>_: Working to initiate the first energy blockchain summit in Septembre. 500 people expected. Prez of projects and hackathon. In Vienna. And then Feb Boston or SF from EG.Tech.  

_<u>Vision</u>_: By 2020 we should have sensors to trace the journey of the energy.Use case grid consention.  Keeping privacy of individuals. There goal building it on new business model. Full open approach through consortium. Building a smart environment through app distribution .

_<u>Who contacted them</u>_: They have been contacted by French company.

_<u>Their analysis</u>_: Big data companies have zero sells around their data like SISCO EG IBM. Killer use case for transmission operator is management of the data to them. They are looking to implement the smartphone business model. Utility lobbies are something they think they can attack. 

Virtual power plants business model as a good example but too big for implementation. 20% of their solution is based on the Blockchain alone. So key and access management are best solved by the blockchain and tokkenisation.

_<u>Next step</u>_: Congress energy in Lyon. Clean tech for Europe. 11 13th 15h00 April. Rieul you could meet with them. > **YES (Rieul's note)**
